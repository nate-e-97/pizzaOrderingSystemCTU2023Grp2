About Us

Our Pizza spot is a beloved location enjoyed by our local community for its bold flavor, rich aroma, and comoforting warmth.

Our Story

Our family loves pizza. However... we weren't in love with the poor quality, pre cooked ingredients that we were putting in our body. We saw this as a chance to give pizza the love it deserves. While sticking with our family roots of locally inspired, and classically crafted food. We dreamed of a pizzeria that brought a warm and cozy ambience to the neighborhood. While are pizzas are simple and easy, we strive for quality. We are also proud to serve a nice selection of locally brewed craft beers. We see our pizza joint as an opportunity to contribute and be a part of the local community.
